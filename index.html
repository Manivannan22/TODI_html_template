ABOUT US

Our Story

Todi is an exciting startup that's making waves in the world of ad tech influencer marketing in India. With its cutting-edge AI technology and data-driven approach, Todi is revolutionizing the way brands connect with influencers and promote their products or services.

But we're not just about technology - we're also passionate about creating authentic and engaging content that resonates with audiences. By focusing on user-generated content and collaborating with inspiring content creators, we help brands connect with their target audiences in a more meaningful and effective way.

Founded in Europe, Todi is now making its mark in the Indian market, empowering brands and social media content creators to create impactful and meaningful marketing campaigns. As a startup with a bold vision and a dedication to innovation, Todi is the perfect partner for brands looking to stay ahead of the curve in the fast-paced world of influencer marketing.

Our Mission and Vision

At Todi, we believe in the power of AI to streamline influencer management and deliver results that are both impactful and measurable.

Our proprietary platform uses sophisticated algorithms and machine learning techniques to optimize outreach, campaign creative development, influencer engagement, content approvals, payments, reporting, and everything in between.
